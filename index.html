About Me

Welcome! I am a postdoctoral researcher in statistics at MOX, Department of Mathematics, Politecnico di Milano.

I obtained my PhD in Data Analytics and Decision Sciences in February 2025 having worked at MOX, Department of Mathematics, Politecnico di Milano and visited the Section of Biostatistics, Department of Public Health, University of Copenhagen.

I am broadly interested in problems at the interface of statistics and real-world applications involving hierarchical data, with a focus on mixed-effects models, survival analysis, clustering, and causal inference.

In my free time, I enjoy DIY activities.
